Description :

This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro.

Ingredients :

  • Avocados - As many as you want
  • Lime - 1/2 Lime per avocado
  • Salt - 1/4 teaspoon per avocado
  • Vegetables - 1/2 onion and 1/2 tomatoes per avocado
  • Herbsand and spices - Cilantro, Garlic and Cayenne pepper to add flavour

Steps :

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together
    in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro,
    and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate
    for 1 hour for improved flavor.
